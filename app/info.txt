GST Notice:
GST notice is sent by tax department.

A user should:
1. Check notice type
2. Check due date
3. Review penalty details
4. Respond before deadline
5. Keep GST documents ready


GST Reply Process:
To reply to GST department:

1. Login to GST portal
2. Open notices section
3. Select notice
4. Upload explanation
5. Submit reply before due date


GST Late Filing:
Late filing may result in:
- penalty
- interest
- legal notice


Wallet Recharge:
Wallet recharge can be done from wallet section.


Call Disconnected Issue:
Call disconnected issue can be solved by:
- checking internet connection
- restarting application
- reconnecting network
